# Hello!

I am a Research Scientist at the [Institute for Replication](https://i4replication.org), where we aim to improve the robustness and reliability of research through a combination of [collaborative events](https://i4replication.org/games/), [tool development](https://ifp.org/the-replication-engine/), and [outreach](https://www.npr.org/2026/02/27/nx-s1-5720653/replication-crisis-games-abel-brodeur). 

Previously, I have conducted research in Bayesian statistics and epidemiology. In a more distant past, I was a public servant and had the opportunity to support various institutions in the Peruvian government with data management, analysis and visualization tasks.
